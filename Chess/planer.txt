Components of Chess:
  - 8x8 board (64 squares)
  - 32 pieces (black/white):
    - 16 pieces per player:
      - 8 pawns
      - 2 knights
      - 2 bishops
      - 2 rooks
      - 1 queen
      - 1 knight
  - 2 players
  - Save feature
  - Resign feature
  - Draw feature

  - Game

Chess/
